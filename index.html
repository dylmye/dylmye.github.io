About DYLAN

Dylan Myers has been using Photoshop since he was three years old. He is well-equipped with his self-taught knowledge of HTML and CSS. He takes after his father with his technological prowess. Dylan is influenced by Design With Intent and frequently uses minimalism in his works, notably his popular Stylish re-work and the design for his recently-launched games website, DAYLOstar. He's also contributed to other people's projects on GitHub.
In terms of Social Media management, Dylan is relatively new to the field, however since the alpha release of DAYLOstar he has been maintaining a Twitter, Facebook and App.Net profile to provide helpful and regular info and drops related to it. He's also worked with upcoming technology news website, Technom, to provide social media solutions indefinitely across popular networks.

Dylan believes in:

  • Semantic Coding
  • Design with intent
  • Automation
  • Cross-device and beautiful
